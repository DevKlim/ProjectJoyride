Balatro-inspired racing game where...

A circuit of 8 maps and 3 laps each. At the start of each lap, enter the repair shop and buy an upgrade to car. Relies on karting stats: Speed, Accel, Turn, Grip, Weight.

Each shop offers 3 stickers to buy with option to reroll with money. A car can have a max of 4 stickers. Shop also allows for maintainence tickets that will upgrade a car part (Engine(Speed), Chassis(Weight), Wheels(Grip), Controls(Turn), Steering(Accel)).

There are also characters with special abilities and passives to choose from. (Stat boosts or abilities)

The race have item boxes to select through a myriad of cool abilities to use like boosts and coins.

Tracks will have coins to collect and maps are randomized.

Two gamemodes are time trial and grand prix.

Lets make time trial first. Each map there is a timer to beat and you have 3 laps. With each map, the timer will reduce starting from 3 mins. It goes 3, 2.75, 2.5, 2.25, 2, 1.75, 1.5, 1.25 minutes. Then player can enter endless where the next race you must beat in 1 minute, then each decrease of the time increments by 10 seconds. When it gets to 20 seconds, keep dividing the time by 2 until Player loses. On the 3rd lap of each map, there will be a special event like environment hazard(making everything slippery) or slower accels.

The kart has Mario Kart controls: tricking, drifting, braking, using item and using ability. Player can reverse and look behind as well.

Camera will be automated and controlled by game, behind the car.

Plans to procedurally generate the map.

Karts initially are all the same but throughout the course of a game, the kart will change. Only thing customizable is the character and its ability.
